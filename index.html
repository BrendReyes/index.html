HOW TO PLAY

Match and Math

- Start the game and choose what difficulty you want.
- Random of cards will appear and you must find the right pair of each card.
- For example: 4*4 was your first flip, your second flip should be 16.
- Your objective is to find all the matching pairs of each card. Having a mismatch has a punishment and vice versa.
- Remember that your in-game actions will be recorded. The number of Flips, Matches, Mismatches are counted.
- Take note that your time is limited, don't let the bomb explode.
